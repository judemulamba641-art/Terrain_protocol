# ⚠️ Risk Model

## Risk Objectives

- Protect liquidity providers
- Prevent bad debt
- Manage NFT volatility
- Enable sustainable leverage

---

## Key Metrics

### Loan-to-Value (LTV)

- Base LTV: 40–60%
- Dynamic adjustment based on risk score

---

### Health Factor (HF)

- HF < 1 → liquidatable

---

## NFT Risk Factors

| Factor        | Impact |
|--------------|--------|
| Rarity       | ↓ risk |
| Liquidity    | ↑ risk |
| Price Vol.   | ↑ risk |
| Terrain Size | ↓ risk |

---

## Dynamic Risk Score

RiskScore affects:
- Max LTV
- Liquidation threshold
- Interest rate premium

---

## Liquidation Model

- Partial liquidation (up to 50%)
- Liquidation bonus: 5–10%
- DAO-adjustable parameters

---

## Oracle Risk Mitigation

- TWAP pricing
- Off-chain + on-chain aggregation
- Update delay protections
